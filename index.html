Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be home during cleaning?

I actually prefer that clients are not home so I can dedicate their entire time slot to a job well done. Many of my long-term clients trust me as a keyholder, which allows for the most efficient service.

Do you provide cleaning supplies?

Yes, supplies are typically provided by me unless something very specific is needed. I use your vacuum if available and sometimes bring my own vacuum as well for the best results.

Are you insured and licensed?

Yes, I am fully licensed and insured. I can also provide a background check and references upon request for your peace of mind.

What about pets?

I love pets! As an owner of two Mastiffs, I'm comfortable with all kinds of animals. I really enjoy visiting homes with pets and will treat them with the same care and respect as I do your home.

How do you handle repeat business?

For repeat clients, I work on a flat fee basis. Once I've established a history with your home, I develop an efficient process tailored to your needs. My deep cleaning approach keeps your home more manageable between visits.
